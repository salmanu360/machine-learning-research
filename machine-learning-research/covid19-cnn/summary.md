# Research Summary

## Title
A Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) Based Framework for Enhanced Diagnosis and Classification of COVID-19 Pneumonia

## Overview
This research investigates the use of convolutional neural networks for improved diagnosis and classification of COVID-19 pneumonia from imaging data.

## Objective
To improve automated detection performance using a deep learning framework tailored for image classification.

## Method
The approach includes:
- preprocessing medical images
- training a CNN model
- evaluating diagnostic performance
- comparing results against traditional methods

## Contribution
The research highlights the effectiveness of CNNs for automated pneumonia classification and supports the use of deep learning in medical decision support systems.

## Keywords
CNN, deep learning, COVID-19, pneumonia, diagnosis, medical imaging
