# Research Summary

## Title
Optimizing Pneumonia Diagnosis during COVID-19: Utilizing Random Forest for Accurate Classification and Effective Public Health Interventions

## Overview
This research studies the use of Random Forest to improve diagnostic classification for pneumonia cases in the context of COVID-19.

## Objective
To evaluate whether Random Forest can provide reliable classification performance for medical decision support.

## Method
- feature extraction
- data preprocessing
- Random Forest model training
- performance evaluation

## Contribution
The study shows that ensemble learning methods such as Random Forest can provide strong classification performance in healthcare-related prediction tasks.

## Keywords
Random Forest, pneumonia diagnosis, COVID-19, healthcare analytics, classification
